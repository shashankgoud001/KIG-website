About Us

Who are we?

Kharagpur Investments Group is a student-run organization that aims to promote financial literacy and investment education among students.

We believe that investing is a crucial life skill that every student should possess, regardless of their academic background or future career aspirations.

Our Mission

Promoting financial literacy and investment education.

What we do

Provide a platform for students to learn about investing, network with like-minded individuals, and explore different investment opportunities.

We organize various events, workshops, and seminars throughout the year to help students develop their investment skills and knowledge.

Core Values

  1. Education: We believe in providing students with access to the resources and knowledge they need to become confident and successful investors.
  2. Inclusivity: We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone feels valued and respected.
  3. Integrity: We are committed to conducting ourselves with honesty, transparency, and professionalism in all our interactions.
  4. Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and encourage our members to work together to achieve their investment goals.
  5. Innovation: We are always seeking new and innovative ways to educate, engage, and inspire our members.
  6. Growth: We are dedicated to helping our members grow both personally and professionally through investment education, networking, and mentorship.
  7. Responsibility: We recognize the importance of responsible investing and encourage our members to consider the social and environmental impact of their investment decisions.
  8. Passion: We are passionate about finance and investing, and we strive to inspire that same passion in our members.

Join us today and become a part of our community of students who are passionate about finance and investing. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced investor, Kharagpur Investments Group has something for everyone. We look forward to meeting you and helping you achieve your investment goals!
